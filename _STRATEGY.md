## ANGLE PRIORITY TIERS

### TIER 1 - Priority Launch (Hit First)
**Strategic rationale:** Highest emotional intensity, clear urgency, large market segments, ready-to-buy mindset.

1. **3AM_Breakdown**
   - Real-time crisis = highest urgency
   - Emotional collapse state = high engagement
   - Clear immediate need: "Make this stop tonight"

2. **Financial_Bleed**
   - Universal pain across all segments
   - Clear ROI positioning angle
   - Most-aware audience = easier conversion
   - Resentment toward vet costs = fertile ground

3. **Medication_Horror**
   - Fear of side effects = actively seeking alternatives
   - High purchase intent
   - Direct competitive displacement angle (vs Apoquel/Cytopoint)

4. **Worked_Then_Stopped**
   - Desperate for something that lasts
   - Grief + hope = powerful emotional combo
   - Clear product differentiation opportunity

---

### TIER 2 - Strong Follow-Up
**Strategic rationale:** Solid pain + desire, but slightly less urgent or requires more trust-building. Still significant market size.

5. **Last_Attempt**
   - High desperation but smaller segment
   - Final-chance positioning = high emotional stakes
   - May convert best with social proof/testimonials

6. **Blood_On_Couch**
   - Visceral, severe cases (smaller but INTENSE)
   - Graphic imagery = strong pattern interrupt potential
   - May require sensitivity in execution

7. **Vet_Gave_Up**
   - Authority abandonment = actively seeking new solutions
   - Trust repair required in messaging
   - "We have answers vets don't" positioning

8. **Life_Consumed**
   - Burnout segment (growing pain point)
   - "Get your life back" angle
   - Freedom/simplicity as core benefit

---

### TIER 3 - Later Testing
**Strategic rationale:** Niche segments, more complex messaging requirements, lower urgency, or potential objections to navigate.

9. **Failing_Parent**
   - Emotional but less direct product fit
   - Requires validation + solution (two-step message)
   - May work better as iteration after trust is built

10. **Invisible_Monster**
    - Requires more diagnosis/education positioning
    - "We address root cause" angle (needs substantiation)
    - May need to pair with authority/science

11. **Zombie_Dog**
    - Specific to dogs currently on medication
    - Smaller segment (subset of medicated dogs)
    - Strong for warm traffic/retargeting

12. **Diet_Maze**
    - Deep in food allergy hypothesis
    - May resist supplement solution initially
    - Requires reframe: "It's not the food, it's inflammation"

13. **DIY_Disaster**
    - Natural-first mindset (good for product fit)
    - But burned by supplements before = objection handling needed
    - "This one actually works" requires strong proof

## TESTING ROADMAP (Initial Plan)

### Phase 1: Tier 1 Market Validation (Weeks 1-3)
- Launch 2-3 ads per Tier 1 angle
- Mix of UGC + MiniVSL formats
- Goal: Identify which angles resonate strongest with cold traffic
- Variable testing: Speaker, tone, hook psychology

### Phase 2: Iterate Winners + Tier 2 Expansion (Weeks 4-6)
- Double down on winning Tier 1 angles with format/variable changes
- Begin testing Tier 2 angles (1-2 ads each)
- Start building learnings database

### Phase 3: Full Market Coverage + Moonshots (Weeks 7+)
- Test Tier 3 angles
- Experiment with wild card approaches
- Focus on scaling proven winners

**Note:** Timeline is flexible based on budget, creative capacity, and performance signals.

## STRATEGIC DECISIONS LOG

### Decision 1: Focus on Allergy Avatar Only (For Now)
- **Date:** 2026-02-09
- **Rationale:** Allergy market is larger and more desperate than mobility. Establish dominance here first before expanding.
- **Implication:** All 13 angles are within SkinAllergies avatar

### Decision 2: Tier 1 Priority = Urgency + Fear
- **Date:** 2026-02-09
- **Rationale:** Cold traffic converts best when pain is acute and urgent. Tier 1 angles have real-time crisis or active fear driving them.

### Decision 3: Medication Angles = Direct Competitive Strategy
- **Date:** 2026-02-09
- **Rationale:** Medication_Horror + Worked_Then_Stopped + Zombie_Dog all position against Apoquel/Cytopoint. This is our primary competitive displacement strategy.

---

## NOTES & STRATEGIC REMINDERS

- **Awareness levels vary widely across angles.** Tier 1 has mix of problem-aware (3AM) and most-aware (Financial Bleed, Medication Horror). Message accordingly.
- **Medication-related angles (Horror, Zombie, Worked-Then-Stopped) are GOLD** for positioning. These people are already spending $130-300/month. Price objection is lower.
- **DIY_Disaster could surprise us.** Natural-first mindset = perfect fit for supplement. But they're burned out. Proof is everything.
- **Blood_On_Couch is high-risk, high-reward.** Severity = intensity, but we need to be empathetic not exploitative.
