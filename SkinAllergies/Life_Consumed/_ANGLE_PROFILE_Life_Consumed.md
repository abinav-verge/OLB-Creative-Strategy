# ANGLE 9: THE SICK CHILD / LIFE CONSUMED

**Core psychology:** Total lifestyle hijack. Their daily routine, freedom, relationships, and mental bandwidth have been consumed by managing this condition.

**Awareness level:** Varies. Defined not by where they are on the solution path but by how much of their life the condition has consumed.

## Beliefs
- "This is like having a sick child"
- "I don't have a life outside of managing this"
- "I love my dog but I miss my old life"
- "This will never end"

## Experiences (from research)
- Daily: wiping paws after every trip outside, cleaning ears, applying creams/foams
- Weekly: medicated baths (some every 72 hours during flare-ups)
- Constant: mopping floors, washing bedding, vacuuming, changing sheets
- Modifying entire household — can't wear shoes indoors, special detergents, anti-mite sprays, buying a Roomba
- Can't go on vacation — had to cancel trips due to flare-ups
- "Elaborate supplement regimens" — multiple products at different times of day
- Some washing dog's bed daily, steam cleaning floors twice daily

## Emotions
Exhaustion, resentment (guilty resentment), loss of freedom, burnout, "I'm done with this"

## Behaviors
- Describing detailed daily routines with clinical precision
- Comparing their situation to having a chronically ill child
- Expressing resentment then immediately feeling guilty about it
- Organizing entire days around the dog's condition management

## Specific desire
"I want my life back. I want this to be simpler."

## Key language from research
- "Same — consumes your life. Almost like having a sick child"
- "My dog is a full time job with his allergies"
- "Life has been, let's say... hard, since I've gotten them. The expenses, the problems, the freedom I don't have anymore"
- "Currently he's getting bathed 1x a fortnight and eyes and ears are wiped out everyday, that's all I got in the tank atm. I'm done with this"
- "We had to cancel a vacation... a new furuncle popped up and I downright sobbed"
- "The amount of money I've spent... I mop and steam the floors twice a day... wash her bed and all the sheets daily"
- "I can't keep up with him, and he never improves"
